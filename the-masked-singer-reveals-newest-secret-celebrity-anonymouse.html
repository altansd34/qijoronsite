'The Masked Singer' Reveals Newest Secret Celebrity, Anonymouse

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Reading Time: 2 minutes

With The Masked Singer’s tenth season around the corner, the show has finally revealed another competitor. The competitor, named Anonymouse, will be showing off their vocals and grooves in the upcoming season premiere on September 10.

The Masked Singer Introduces Anonymouse

In the show’s most recent post, Anonymouse is shown as a female vocalist sporting a punk-rock red dress and spiky leather jacket. Her outfit is paired up with a knee-high black boots and black leather choker complemented by a red bandana.

“Mice to meet you, #AnonymouseMask! Find out who’s under the mask on a special #TheMaskedSinger Season 10 Kickoff episode coming your way September 10 on @foxtv and next day on @hulu!” the caption reads.

The secret celebrity will take the stage for the first time in the season premiere. This season is said to be “one of the biggest, can’t-miss unmaskings in the show’s history.”

In The Masked Singer’s video sneak peek, Anonymouse is flaunting her vocals with Heart’s “What About Love?” As she hits the high notes of the track, pyrotechnics light up the stage alongside red fired hearts at the back.

The Masked Singer season 10 will also be treating fans with the comeback of several high profile alumni from previous seasons. These celebrities include former Destiny’s Child singer Michelle Williams (Butterfly), *NSYNC ’s Joey Fatone (Rabbit), rapper Bow Wow (Frog), actress Rumer Willis (Lion), NBA player Victor Oladipo (Thingamajig) and MLB pitcher Barry Zito (Rhino).

Fans Think Anonymouse is Demi Lovato

Amid the release of the audio-visual sneak peek, fans quickly turned on their detective mode and began the guessing game. Apparently, many fans believe that Anonymouse could be pop megastar Demi Lovato.

“This is 100% Demi Lovato. So glad she finally decided to do the show,” one fan wrote.

“Hopping on the Demi Train, she sounds so beautiful,” another person commented.

According to many fans, Anonymouse is “definitely” giving Demi Lovato vibes both based on costume and voice. However, some other fans think that the chanteuse could also be Kelly Clarkson.

The Masked Singer will be having a new format this season. Three groups of singers will be facing off ahead of a special Battle Royale during the semi-finals. Furthermore, a single wild card contestant will return to contend against each group.

Jenny McCarthy Wahlberg, Nicole Scherzinger, Ken Jeong and Robin Thicke will remain as the show’s guessers. Nick Cannon will also come back as host for season 10.
