Heidi Klum Stuns in Underwater-Inspired 'Little Mermaid' Premiere Look

August 2024 · 2 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

FilmMagic

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Supermodel and television personality Heidi Klum made waves on the red carpet at the Los Angeles premiere of Disney’s The Little Mermaid. Embracing the theme of the beloved underwater tale, Klum showcased an elegant ensemble that captured the essence of the oceanic world.

Heidi Klum Shows Up in Underwater-Inspired Gown

Donning a mesmerizing gown by Jasmin Erbaş Couture, Klum epitomized an undersea goddess as she graced the premiere of The Little Mermaid in Los Angeles. The lilac creation featured a sculptural flower-like bodice and a captivating floor-sweeping train adorned with silver sequins.

Completing her look, Klum opted for see-through heels that added an dainty touch to her leggy appearance. Klum’s elegant ensemble paid homage to the enchanting world beneath the waves. It reflects the movie’s captivating storyline and celebrates the groundbreaking casting of Halle Bailey as Ariel.

Before making her grand entrance, Klum treated her followers to a behind-the-scenes glimpse of her red carpet preparations. In an Instagram reel, she showcased her impeccable style and infectious energy. Starting with a playful scene of her sunbathing in a cherry-printed bikini while her glam squad worked their magic.

The clip then transitioned to Klum joyfully frolicking in a backyard to the beats of Kali Uchis’ “telepatía,” creating an atmosphere of carefree bliss. The final result of her transformation revealed intricate butterfly appliqués. It includes wing-like flaps adorning the back of her mesmerizing gown, adding a whimsical element to the overall look.

Heidi Klum Kicked Off The Summer Season By Posing Poolside in Nothing But a Brown Thong

Klum knows how to make a splash when it comes to swimwear season. The German supermodel and TV personality recently shared a series of photos on Instagram, showcasing her poolside style. In the snapshots, Klum can be seen lying face down on a towel, wearing nothing but a tiny brown thong and accessorizing with a gold chain necklace and brown sunglasses.

With her long blonde hair strategically covering her chest, Klum exuded confidence and relaxation. The caption, “… and that was Monday,” summed up her carefree attitude.

Shortly after the revealing poolside photos, Klum also shared a video of herself in a different bikini, getting ready for the premiere of Disney’s The Little Mermaid. From thong to red carpet glamour, Klum proved she can effortlessly transition between swimwear and high-fashion looks.
