Here’s an in-depth look into each of the ‘Britain’s Got Talent’ judges. How well do you actually know David Walliams, Alesha Dixon, Amanda Holden and Simon Cowell?

How well do you know the BGT judges?

David Walliams Net Worth

David Walliams is well known for his fun and crude behavior on BGT and manages to win the award for the nation’s favorite TV judge every year! Walliams first rose to fame with his well-renowned show, Little Britain and then continued to show off his success with an ongoing collection of popular children’s books. The BGT judge has even hosted award shows and notable TV events in the past, making him the perfect fit for the UK’s favorite talent show!

NET WORTH: $25 MILLION

Simon Cowell Net Worth

Simon Cowell is the biggest name when it comes to the ‘Got Talent’ franchise, but it’s no surprise, he created it. The TV judge has a long history of signing world-famous artists and discovering amazing talent on both BGT & AGT. Simon wasn’t the most well-behaved kid so the fact that he takes home millions every year is a story worth remembering. Cowell was first discovered worldwide when he appeared as a judge on Pop Idol and later American Idol, where he built up his well-known persona.

NET WORTH: $600 MILLION

Amanda Holden Net Worth

Amanda Holden is no stranger to Britain’s Got Talent. She’s appeared on every single episode to date ever since it began in the UK. Holden is also known for her role in films such as ‘Wild at Heart’ and ‘Jonathan Creek’ as well as her various roles presenting on television over the years. The TV personality has also given being an author and singing a go, just a few more things to add to her life achievements list!

NET WORTH: $6 MILLION

Alesha Dixon Net Worth

Alesha Dixon has done it all, from winning Strictly Come Dancing back in 2007 to signing a record contract with Asylum Records. Dixon first joined the BGT lineup back in 2012 and hasn’t looked back. She previously hosted Cowell’s former dancing show on the BBC, ‘The Greatest Dancer’ and appeared as a judge on ‘AGT: The Champions’ earlier this year. Alesha Dixon likes to stay busy!

NET WORTH: $4 MILLION
