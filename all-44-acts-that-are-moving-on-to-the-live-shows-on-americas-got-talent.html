Where Is Shevon Nieto?

Shevon Nieto had one of the most memorable auditions of the entire competition and we labeled her as the frontrunner. However, she is visibly absent from this list. While the other contestants have confirmed that they would be moving on to the live shows, Shevon has remained very mum about the show. Her recent pregnancy announcement may have something to do with that as it may prove to be too risky for her to travel for the live shows if needed.

Other acts that did not make it past Judge Cuts include singer Ashley Marina who Simon made sing three times during her audition and comedians Marty Ross and Crystal Powell. It really is anyone’s chance to win this season now that there is no clear frontrunner.
