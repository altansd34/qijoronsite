Gina Miles Revisits Her 'The Voice' Blind Audition Song in New Cover

August 2024 · 2 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Voice

Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Gina Miles, the winner of The Voice season 23, recently posted a cover of her Blind Audition song, “The One That Got Away” by Katy Perry, on Instagram. The video has already racked up thousands of views in just a few days and a lot of praise from fans.

Gina Miles Sings an Acoustic Version of Her Blind Audition Song

In the video, Miles is seen strumming a guitar and singing the song in a stripped-down version. Her vocals are just as powerful as they were on the show, and she delivers the emotional lyrics with conviction.

Fans in the comments were quick to praise Miles’s vocals, with many calling her performance “stunning” and “breathtaking.”

“You wanna know what heaven sounds like just listen to Gina Miles sing,” One fan commented.

“Slayed again just like the first time,” another fan commented.

Ever since winning The Voice, Miles has continued to pursue her love of singing, and she has even performed at a graduation ceremony for firefighters. She has also spoken about her hopes for the future, and she says that she wants to make music that is “authentic and real” to her.

Miles has been very active on social media since winning The Voice. She has posted videos of herself singing covers of popular songs, and she has also shared some of her original music. Miles has also been using her platform to raise awareness for important causes, such as mental health and bullying.

She Sounded Even Better Then Before, According to Fans

Miles’s cover of “The One That Got Away” is a reminder of her incredible vocal range and her ability to connect with audiences on an emotional level. What’s even more impressive is that fans are saying that she sounded even better than she did on the show.

“I’m not super crazy confident normally, but when I sing, I’m a very different person. A lot of people are shocked by the contrast of my singing voice and my normal voice,” Miles said.

Miles’s new cover of “The One That Got Away” is a sign of great things to come. She is a talented singer with a bright future ahead of her. Her music has a unique way of resonating with listeners, leaving an unforgettable mark on their hearts.
