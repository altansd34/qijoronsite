A Deep Dive Into Sharna Burgess and Brian Austin Green's Relationship Timeline

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Photo by: Eric McCandless/ABC via Getty Images

Reading Time: 5 minutes

When Sharna Burgess and Brian Austin Green first met, they didn’t think the spark would last. However, as they got along, chemistry proved to be undeniable. The duo quarantined together during the pandemic, inescapably cultivating romance they plan to treasure forever.

From having a casual coffee date to making their love official, Burgess and Green have an interesting fairy tale. Here’s everything we know about the DWTS pair’s relationship from beginning to now.

October 2020: Sharna Burgess, Brian Austin Green Meet for a Coffee Date

For their first date, Burgess and Green went out for coffee after they were set up by their mutual business manager. According to the Don’t Blink Actor, he and the DWTS star “completely lost track of time” during this meeting and the next. Nevertheless, Green later cleared up that the both of them were simply testing the waters before.

December 2020: Burgess Reveals She’s Already Off the Market

In an interview with Us Weekly, Burgess announced that she’s not anymore looking for a beau. By late December, Burgess and Green sparked romance rumors for the first time, after they were seen boarding a flight together at the Los Angeles International (LAX) Airport. This was also the time when Green filed for divorce from Megan Fox.

January 2021: They Spend Vacation Together, Make Their Relationship Instagram Official

The two further ignited dating rumors after they uploaded similar photos during their getaway. Green then addressed the speculation, stating that Burgess is an “amazing woman” but they don’t have “any labels for anything” yet. After some days though, the two came clean and announced their relationship via Instagram.

February 2021: They Celebrate Their First Valentine’s Day

Through an Instagram Live, the new couple celebrated their first Valentine’s Day together. Burgess and Green particularly opened up about their relationship and the positive impact it brought in their lives. According to Green, the romance was a great way to kick off 2021, believing that it was the “start of something amazing.”

March 2021: Burgess, Green Make Their Public Debut

Supporting a good cause at Cassie Scerbo’s birthday party, the two officially went out as loving partners for the event. In the following month, Green admitted that their similar moral compass is what makes their connection special. 

July 2021: Burgess Bonds with Green, His Sons at Walt Disney World

Clearly, Burgess is also supportive of Green’s relationship with his sons. Two years back, the two shared snaps and video clips of their family bonding at Walt Disney World. In the clip, the group was enjoying their walk in the park.

September 2021: They Compete on Dancing With The Stars 

Burgess and Green entered Dancing With The Stars as a real couple, the first pair to enter the season already in love. For their first dance, they graced the ballroom with a foxtrot routine and sealed the same with an adorable kiss. The duo walked out of the competition during week four of their DWTS season.

October 2021: They Celebrate One Year Since They Met

After a year of sparking connection, the romantic duo celebrated the first anniversary of their meeting. To commemorate the occasion, Green posted a photo of them kissing at the Original Immersive Van Gogh Exhibit in Los Angeles on Instagram.

February 2022: Burgess, Green Reveal Their Pregnancy

The DWTS couple announced that they were already expecting their first born together. Alongside the good news, they shared their sweet photo taken in Hawaii, while Burgess is proudly showing off her baby bump. They subsequently announced that they would be welcoming a baby boy to the family. 

April 2022: Green Pays Tribute to Burgess for Taking Care of Him Amid Health Scare

Last year, Green went candid with his followers and shared that he had been ill for more than six weeks with an ulcerative colitis flare-up. On Instagram, he praised Burgess for helping him recover and for being there for his children. 

June 2022: They Welcomed Their First Child Together

After celebrating 35 weeks of her pregnancy in May 2022, the two welcomed their first son together, Zane Walker Green. According to Burgess during her appearance on Good Morning America, she’s excited for a new version of her that’s “nurturing, caring, dedicated.”

October 2022: They Celebrate Second Anniversary of Their Meetup

Over a week after they attended Burgess’s U.S. citizenship ceremony, the couple celebrated another milestone. Burgess and Green celebrated two years of the day they first met. On Instagram, Green penned a sweet letter expressing his gratitude for the DWTS pro. As stated, “The life that we have created and continue to create is better than anything I ever thought was possible.”

December 2022: They Celebrate Zane’s First Christmas

Zane spent his first Christmas alongside his lovely parents. Burgess shared the special moment on Instagram, posting their family photo with Santa.

March 2023: The Couple Went on a Quick Trip to Las Vegas

The DWTS pair went to Las Vegas to spend a quick vacation together. On Instagram, Burgess shared some of their romantic snaps. One photo showed Green and her posing on a gold couch at a nightclub. Another showed the couple leaning in to share a sweet smooch.

July 2023: Burgess Honors Green on His 50th Birthday

Celebrating Green’s 50th birthday, Burgess threw a surprise party for him at their home in Los Angeles. She also penned a long post about her beau on Instagram. According to the DWTS star, Green is “the most kind, loyal to a fault, generous, and all round good man” she’s ever known. She further expressed her love for the actor, saying that Green is the right amount of sarcasm that her Aussie humor needs.
