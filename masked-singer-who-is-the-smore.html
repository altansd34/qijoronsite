The Masked Singer is back for its TENTH season! And if you would’ve told me we’d be here when the show started in 2019, I don’t know that I would’ve believed you. This season is guaranteed to be epic! Even the costumes have leveled up, including the S’more, who’s taking the stage for the premiere and melting hearts.

But who is the S’more? Let’s go through everything we know so far about this sweet treat’s identity.

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 10

The S’more kicked off his “swashbuckling” clues by saying that he “blazes his own trail.” He talked about his “rocket” to fame, while we saw a rocket take off in the background. The S’more went from obscurity to “major heartthrob status” in weeks. He mentioned that millions cheered him on, while he crawled toward a tip jar. And “step by step,” he even became a household name.

Then, he talked about making headlines with “fellow adventurers and idols” in places near and far. The camera zoomed in on cans of “Mashed Soda” and a Chicago deep dish pizza next. The S’more was then seen next to a Christmas tree and we got a close-up of a golden angel on top. He explained that “when [he] finished with [his] travels, [he] hung up his cape.” After the S’more’s first performance, we got one last clue. It was a movie reel that said “Starring S’more.”

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer S’more

The judges thought that the S’more could be Joey McIntyre from New Kids on the Block, Justin Guarini, or Kevin Richardson from the Backstreet Boys. Online, guesses are centered around boy banders too! Many predictions agree with Kevin Richardson or Joey McIntyre, but other popular names include AJ McLean from BSB and Ashley Parker Angel from O-Town. With the Justin Guarini guess, could we really see another American Idol runner-up on The Masked Singer after David Archuleta’s appearance last season?

Who is the S’more on The Masked Singer?

The clues and voice all point to this being one person! So we have to predict that the S’more’s true celebrity identity is…

Ashley Parker Angel!

Not only does the voice point to him, but the clues do too. And here’s how! To start, there are a lot of mentions about the S’more going from “obscurity to major heartthrob status in weeks,” becoming a “household name,” and how “millions cheered him on.” These all make sense in the context of him rising to fame as a member of O-Town on Making the Band. He also talked about working with an “industry legend,” which could apply to Lou Pearlman, who formed NSYNC and the Backstreet Boys, or Clive Davis, who the boy band later signed with. S’more also mentioned “tasting victory before” which would make sense here.

The icy tip jar points to Ashley’s signature frosted tips. He mentioned making headlines with fellow idols, and O-Town toured with Britney Spears. We saw a soda can, which is a nod to pop music, and a Chicago deep dish pizza, which is a reference to his role in the movie Pizza Man. A TV with an “M” on it refers to MTV, where Making the Band and his show There and Back both aired. S’more mentioned conquering the Broadway stage and we saw a cauldron, both alluding to him being in Wicked on Broadway.

In the clues we also saw a game console, which harkens back to him voicing Alex in Lunar: The Silver Star. The orange clue makes sense too, since O-Town got their start in Orlando, Florida. And, in case you missed it, there was a literal ANGEL in his first clue package, an homage to his name.

The Masked Singer excitement continues Wednesday nights on FOX! Don’t miss the S’more and even more costumed celebrities singing for their lives every week.
