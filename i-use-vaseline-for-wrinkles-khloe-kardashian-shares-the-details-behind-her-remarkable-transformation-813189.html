I Use Vaseline for Wrinkles, Khlo Kardashian Shares the Details Behind Her Remarkable Transf

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Despite being a successful entrepreneur, Khloé Kardashian doesn’t believe in wasting money on expensive beauty treatments. Her stunning transformation over the years is a testament to her use of affordable tricks that have become vital in her beauty routine. Khloé openly shares these budget-friendly tips she uses to maintain her breathtaking appearance.

She uses Vaseline as her eye cream.

Vaseline can effectively substitute costly makeup removers and effortlessly remove mascara and eyeliner from your face. It is also gentle enough to be used on the delicate skin of your eyelids, providing nourishment and hydration.

The reality TV star openly admits to incorporating this budget-friendly beauty product into her skincare routine to maintain soft and moisturized skin around her eyes. “I wear Vaseline around my eyes every night. I think it helps with wrinkles and all that. [It’s] really inexpensive and easy,” she said.

She reapplies sunscreen regularly.

Wearing sunscreen is crucial for maintaining smooth and youthful skin in the long run, as it helps prevent wrinkles. However, it is insufficient to apply sunscreen just once a day. In fact, experts recommend reapplying it every two to three hours.

Khloé Kardashian affirms that she never leaves her house without sunscreen, prioritizing its use as part of her skincare routine. “I have sunscreen in all of my cars. Like in [all] the cup holder[s] to remind me,” she said. “I’m also very into putting sunscreen on my hands when I’m in the car—I don’t want to get sunspots,” she added.

She adds collagen to her coffee.

Taking collagen regularly can potentially enhance the overall quality of your skin and help delay signs of aging. However, it is essential to consult with your healthcare provider beforehand, as certain forms of collagen may trigger allergic reactions.

Khloé Kardashian says she has a habit of incorporating collagen into her morning coffee. “It’s literally a no-brainer; it doesn’t feel like you have an added step. It’s essentially [like] if you poured sugar in your coffee,” she shared.

She uses natural makeup removers.

Cleansing your face before bedtime is essential for preventing various skin issues, but it is crucial to opt for a high-quality, mild makeup remover. Some makeup removers containing artificial ingredients can potentially irritate sensitive skin, leading to itching and redness.

The reality TV star openly shares her preference for using gentler alternatives to traditional makeup removers when cleansing her skin. “I use either baby oil or coconut oil to take off my makeup,” she said.

Despite being a stunning and accomplished woman, Khloé Kardashian has openly acknowledged her ongoing battle with insecurities. The 38-year-old candidly shared with her Instagram followers that she constantly faces comparisons to her famous sisters, which has a significant impact on her self-esteem.
