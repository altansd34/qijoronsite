I Thought It Was Your Sister, People Cant Get Over Naomi Campbells Mom Who Seems as Youn

August 2024 · 2 minute read

As the world recently tried to grasp the news of Naomi Campbell becoming a happy mother at 50, many people are eager to learn more about the model’s mom, who brought her up and passed some good genes down to her daughter. Valerie Morris, who’s now 70 years old, stuns her daughter’s fans with an incredibly youthful look, and we’d love for you to be amazed at her never-fading beauty together with us.

The mother-daughter duo shares an incredible bond.

Valerie Morris gave birth to the future supermodel Naomi Campbell when she was only 19. The young mom then worked as a dancer in a band touring the Middle East and Europe. Not much time had passed after the birth of her daughter before she got back into the business.

Morris had to leave a young Campbell in Streatham with her mother, but she always sent money to pay for her daughter’s private education.

Campbell and Morris now have a very warm and close relationship. In one of her posts dedicated to her mom’s birthday, the model wrote, “Thank you for bringing me into this world. And for standing by my side unconditionally at all times. May this be the most amazing year of good health, love, and blessings, Ma,” she wrote. She also added, “I love you so much.”

Fans immediately noticed that the mom and daughter looked almost the same age.

After Campbell introduced her mom to the public, people just couldn’t believe her real age. 67 at the time, Morris was so young-looking that some people wrote that they thought she was Campbell’s sister.

Fans just couldn’t pass by without noticing the smashing game of genes that make up this family. “If her mom looks that young, then Naomi is going to look 30 for another 30 years,” they commented.

Morris is not only young in her looks, but in her spirits too.

Naomi Campbell began modeling when she was just 7. Her mom has always been her biggest supporter and mentor, and she was very much into beauty and fashion, just like her daughter.

“When I was 12 or 13, she appeared in a fashion show and I remember her showing me how to walk in the hallway at home,” Campbell recalled in an interview. “Lionel Richie was playing in the background and I watched her walk up and down to the beat of the music.”

Valerie Morris hasn’t lost her incredible fashion and beauty sense since that time. At 70 years old, the woman is a model herself and is known for her glamorous and fashionable Instagram account. She posts photos where she rocks posh gowns, and people just can’t take their eyes off her. And with the arrival of Campbell’s first baby, there is no doubt that Morris will be the most loving and beautiful grandma to her cute granddaughter!
