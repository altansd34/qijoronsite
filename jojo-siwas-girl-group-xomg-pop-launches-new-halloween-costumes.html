JoJo Siwas Girl Group XOMG Pop Launches New Halloween Costumes

August 2024 · 2 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

XOMG Pop via Instagram

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Teen pop group XOMG Pop has announced their latest commercial project this year. Apparently, the girl band is launching a colorful set of Halloween costumes amid fall.

XOMG Pop Announces New Halloween Project

Before January ended, XOMG Pop dropped a great news for their fans. Seemingly, the pop group is thinking ahead of time as they have already designed a costume for Halloween. On Instagram, the group posted a photo of the colorful piece, a glittery and festive jacket with a popping “XOMG” stapled in front.

“It’s only January but we are officially sooooo excited for Halloween!!!!!!!! XOMG POP! costumes at Walmart! Comment if your excited too!” they wrote.

In the comment section, their members have expressed excitement over the project. Kinley Cunningham stated that she’s anticipating their fans to wear the costume during the holiday.

“I cannot wait for these costumes to come out!!! Dream come true!” Cunningham wrote. “Cannot wait to see you all dressed like XOMGPOP next HALLOWEEN!!!!!”

Brooklynn Pitts mentioned that the costumes are “crazy cool.” Leigha Rose Sanderson, another XOMG Pop member, admitted that she has never been happier and more excited with a project.

Former Member Kiya is Now Part of KIDZ BOP

Last September 2022, 13-year-old Kiya Barczyszyn abruptly left XOMG Pop for unknown reasons. Such departure, unfortunately, has not been addressed by JoJo Siwa and her mother Jessalynn.

Be that as it may, it seems that Barczyszyn has been doing well at present. She recently announced that she’s already a member of musician ensemble KIDZ BOP.

“BIG NEWS! I’m SO excited to announce that I’m a KIDZ BOP Kid AND I’m going on TOUR this summer!” she wrote on Instagram.

Barczyszyn is set to embark on tour this year alongside other members of the group. KIDZ BOP Never Stop Tour will begin on June 24 at The Palace Theater in Stamford, Connecticut and will end on September 14 at the Washing State Fair in Puyallup, Washington.
