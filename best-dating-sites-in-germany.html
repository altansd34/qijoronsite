    Top 10 German dating websites in 2022

    Swiping left or right on other people’s profile pictures has long since become the standard principle of many dating apps.

    Of course, Tinder and other internationally renowned matchmaking services like Bumble, Happn or OKCupid are available in Germany as well with plenty of profiles.

    But for our following list, we’ve selected websites that originate in Germany or are particularly popular there.

    1. Parship

    Parship has been around since 2001 and claims to successfully connect people in close to 40% of cases. Their matchmaking principle is based on a comprehensive personal profile for which you answer 80 questions with hundreds of choices.

    Your partnership personality is based on values, attitudes and interests relevant to your relationship. You’ll receive many suggestions for accurate matches and can make use of additional content such as dating and relationship advice or premium services and coaching.

    Parship is free to use though has a limit on how many messages you can send. Premium subscriptions are available starting at €45 per month.

    2. Finya

    Finya was founded in 2001 in Hamburg. It’s one of the few dating websites which is completely free to use and will therefore show you ads.

    Thanks to hundreds of thousands of profiles, Finya can offer you tailored search results and match suggestions, though you can also discover other users in a playful manner.

    Contact to other members happens through messages, chat or by sending hearts. Finya has received numerous rewards throughout the years.

    3. Lablue

    Lablue targets singles above the age of 30 and has been around since 2002. There is no fee for using the platform, which currently has around 500,000 active users.

    All profiles require registration through email and are checked manually, so fake profiles are hardly an issue. The Lablue website design is a bit vanilla, but all the necessary features such as search, chat and browsing user profiles are available.

    The service caters to singles looking for serious, long-term relationships instead of casual flirts or affairs.

    4. Lovescout24

    The average age on Lovescout24 is just below 35 years. Though it’s a premium service, the platform is slightly cheaper than the competition and monthly memberships start as low as €40.

    Lovescout24 boasts one million successful connections and one million active monthly users with a database of more than five million singles.

    You can create a highly detailed profile and benefit from a comprehensive search feature, which delivers accurate results. You also get helpful advice, coaching and even live events organized by the platform.

    In case you’ve found your match or want to leave Lovescout24 for any other reason, canceling your subscription and deleting your profile are also hassle-free. The platform uses the same design and offers the identical service as Neu.de.

    5. Dating Cafe

    The service of Dating Cafe has been around for more than 20 years, as you can tell by its design. Nonetheless, you can create a comprehensive profile and use excellent features to search the database of close to two million subscribers.

    You can try the premium service for free, but membership starts at €15 per month. Dating Cafe targets singles around the age of 30, though you’ll definitely find users above that age as well.

    You can contact others by sending a message or giving likes. Apart from the online services, Dating Cafe offers single events and vacations.  

    6. Single

    Single.de allows you to register and keep a basic profile for free, but the premium membership starts at €25 for three months. The site claims two million German profiles and around three thousand new members daily.

    You have sufficient options to create an individual profile and the extensive search is certainly helpful in finding someone matching your interests.

    The platform manually checks profiles and has a personal single feed for you to browse.

    7. ElitePartner

    ElitePartner specifically targets academics, so the average user age is slightly higher when compared to other platforms. The number of roughly four million members proves that EliteParnter is definitely filling the niche of dating people with a university degree or academic background.

    A search on ElitePartner offers many filtering options and the suggested matches are highly accurate. You can also use their mobile app, which includes the possibility of searching for people near you. All this comes at an elite price, however: memberships start at €70 per month.

    8. eDarling

    Similar to ElitePartner, eDarling caters to singles with a high level of education. The platform is available in all of Europe with a total number of more than 13 million members, roughly three of which hail from Germany.

    Upon registration, you’re presented with a comprehensive questionnaire of more than 250 questions to improve the quality of your matches.

    Virtually meeting someone includes messages as well as smiles and likes. There’s also an option of seeing users who are excluded by the matching algorithm. An eDarling subscription starts at €30 per month.

    9. Bildkontakte

    Most singles rely on pictures to find a possible partner online. Bildkontakte puts that feature front and center: every profile needs to be represented by an appropriate photo.

    The website’s team manually checks each profile to weed out any fakes. The service boasts 50,000 daily active users and four million members.

    You can contact other users after registration without having to pay, but premium features are only available with a monthly subscription starting at €10.

    10. Lovoo

    Lovoo allows you to register on their website, but the service primarily consists of a smartphone app for Android and iOS with in-app purchases for premium features.

    Their location search is called “live radar” and shows you other users in your vicinity. The ghost mode enables you to browse profiles incognito.

    Matches work by swiping left or right on user profiles, but unlike Tinder, you can contact others without having to match first. Lovoo has been around since 2011.
