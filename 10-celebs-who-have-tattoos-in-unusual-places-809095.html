10 Celebs Who Have Tattoos in Unusual Places

August 2024 · 3 minute read

A lot of tattoos carry a special meaning or significant story. They can tell stories about past decisions, be reminders of one’s ancestors, or even show the unbreakable bond between family and friends. And most importantly, tattoos can be found in the least expected places.

Bright Side has compiled a list of 10 celebrity tattoos that are in the most surprising places. So let’s go through the list and find out more about our favorite celebrities.

1. Jason Momoa recently showed off a new tattoo he got on the side of his head.

With this tattoo, the Aquaman actor recalls his Hawaiian roots as his father is Native Hawaiian. In Polynesian culture, the placement of a tattoo can have significant meaning, and a tattoo on the head can relate to one’s “spirituality” and “knowledge” of their heritage.

2. Dakota Johnson has a “look at the moon” tattoo surrounded by stars.

3. Brooklyn Beckham has a “mama’s boy” tat on his chest.

4. Demi Lovato has a giant spider on the side of their head.

B4859 / Avalon/Photoshot/East News

They later explained the inspiration behind the tattoo via their Instagram story, writing, “It was Grandmother Spider who taught us many things. She taught us about pottery and weaving. She taught us about fire and light and dark. She taught us that we are all connected on the web, each one of us having our place in this world.”

5. Kendall Jenner’s “meow” lip tat

On The Ellen DeGeneres Show, Jenner revealed that she got that tattoo suddenly. “I was not thinking clearly,” she said. “The word ’meow,’ she added, “was literally the first thing that came to my mind.”

6. David Beckham’s then-4-year-old daughter designed a tattoo for him.

She drew a girl wearing a dress and high heels with her arms outstretched, looking like she’s about to give her dad a big hug.

7. Post Malone has “Always Tired” underneath his eyes.

8. Priyanka Chopra has a “Daddy’s lil girl...” on her right arm.

ANGELA WEISS/AFP/East News

Priyanka said about her tattoo: “Ever since I was a kid, I always wanted to be like my dad. I was like, “I want to be the person who’s the funniest in the room. I want to be on stage and own it like he does. I want to be excellent at surgery. I want my life to mean something.’” The tattoo is in her father’s handwriting.

9. Aaron Carter has a Rihanna face tat.

This is the first tattoo Aaron has that covers his entire face despite the fact that he already has several on his body, including his neck.

10. Lady Gaga has a David Bowie tattoo on her body.

On February 13, 2016, just one month after David Bowie died, Gaga added a portrait of the legendary singer above her left hip. Gaga talks about Bowie as one of her main influences and told The Hollywood Reporter shortly after his death, “He is a true, true artist and I don’t know if I ever went, ’Oh, I’m going to be that way like this,’ or if I arrived upon it slowly, realizing it was my calling and that’s what drew me to him.”

Which of these celeb tattoos surprised you the most? Do you have tattoos? Share them down in the comment section and tell us your story!
