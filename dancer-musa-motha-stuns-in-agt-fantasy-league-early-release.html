AGT: Fantasy League has shared another early release video from Monday night’s new episode of the show, featuring BGT star Musa Motha. The dancer showed off some incredible moves in an inspiring routine that had the judges on their feet.

Musa Motha Performs in AGT: Fantasy League Early Release

Musa Motha earned the group Golden Buzzer on Britain’s Got Talent Series 16, making it all the way to fifth place. Motha shared that he grew up wanting to be an athlete, but he was diagnosed with cancer at a young age and had to have his leg amputated. That didn’t stop him from becoming a dancer.

As host Terry Crews shared with the audience, Motha was unable to be there in person for AGT: Fantasy League, so he performed remotely from London. His mentor, judge Simon Cowell advised him to perform solo this time, rather than having other dancers on stage.

That’s exactly what Motha did in his uplifting performance, in which he utilized his crutch as part of the dance routine. As he was dancing, positive comments from the BGT judges could be heard. The Fantasy League judges gave the routine a standing ovation.

What Did the Judges Have to Say?

The judges had plenty of praise for Motha after his performance. Cowell told him, “Even though it was remote, it was close. Honestly, I have so much respect for you as a person, as an artist. You are exceptional.”

Howie Mandel told Motha that he’s “reached such heights,” adding, “There are people with twice as many legs as you that don’t come close to what I just witnessed right here.”

Mel B shared that she trained as a dancer, but she “didn’t have half as much control or technique that you have.” She added that “we need to see more of this.” Mel also made sure to point out that Motha is “really cute.”

Find out if Motha made it through to the next round of the competition when AGT: Fantasy League returns on Monday night at 8/7c on NBC.
