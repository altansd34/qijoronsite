12 Celebrities Who Handled Wardrobe Malfunctions Spectacularly

August 2024 · 3 minute read

When it comes to celebrities, their outfits play a crucial role in transforming them into stunning beauty icons. From red-carpet events to stage performances, every opportunity is a chance for them to shine. But even in the glamorous world of show business, mishaps can happen since no one is spared from unexpected surprises. At the end of the day, it’s how they handle it that makes them true icons.

1. Hayley Atwell on the BAFTAs red carpet

Choosing her figure-hugging dress Atwell was sure to turn heads at the event. Though her beautiful gown seemed to have some other plans splitting right before the cameras. She knew the show must go on so decided not to pay so much attention and took to the stage to present an award no matter what.

2. Cardi B at the Bonnaroo Music and Arts Festival

Invision/Invision/East News, Invision/Invision/East News

In 2019, while the American singer was performing at the Bonnaroo Music and Arts Festival, the rhinestone gown she was wearing split open in the back. Cardi B didn’t let that problem ruin her performance, so she went backstage and then returned wearing a white robe to continue her show.

3. Halle Berry while attending the Revlon Love Is On Million Dollar Challenge

Jack Shea/Starshots/Broadimage/EAST NEWS

It’s still not clear what caused the sweaty slip this time since the weather was fine that evening. Nevertheless, Berry didn’t allow it to stop her from smiling for the cameras since the event was held to give a charity a one million dollar donation.

4. Dakota Johnson on the red carpet at the Venice Film Festival

Film director Luca Guadagnino saved actress Dakota Johnson from a major embarrassment because of a little slip with her outfit. It seems that the celebrity’s dress slipped down a bit, almost exposing her braless chest. Luca luckily noticed it before the photographers did.

5. Katy Perry at the 2017 Academy Awards

After the 2017 Academy Awards, most celebrities gathered at a celebratory party hosted by Vanity Fair, including Katy Perry, who had a misfortunate accident that made her the victim of an embarrassing wardrobe malfunction: it left the back of her body a bit exposed.

6. Gigi Hadid on the runway at Marc Jacobs’ NYFW show

Before Gigi Hadid took to the runway at fashion designer Marc Jacobs’ 2019 New York Fashion Week show, her heels broke. Still, the model showed all her confidence and walked barefoot on the catwalk, as if she were wearing her shoes.

7. Beyoncé during the 2005 Academy Awards

During a mid-performance costume change at the 2005 Academy Awards, Beyoncé lost a shoe in her dress, so she balanced on a high heel and stood on her tiptoes barefoot. The only thing going through her mind at the time was, “Oh my God, shoe, please stay in my dress!”

8. Priyanka Chopra on the red carpet at the 2019 Cannes Film Festival

Minutes before Priyanka Chopra was due to hit the red carpet at the 2019 Cannes Film Festival, the zipper on her dress broke, so her team had to stitch the garment on during the 5-minute drive to the venue.

9. Miley Cyrus during NBC’s live special Miley’s New Year’s Eve Party

After singing a couple of songs on a live special, Miley Cyrus’ wardrobe top ripped in the middle of the stage. The singer simply turned around, dropped the garment, sang her way out of everyone’s sight, and then returned with a red blazer to continue the show.

10. Kylie Jenner at the 2018 Met Gala

The dress Kylie Jenner wore to the 2018 Met Gala ripped when she was putting it on just before leaving for the event, so her team had to fix the issue and quickly add a zipper.

11. Margot Robbie at the European Premiere of Tarzan

Stephen Lock / i-Images/EAST NEWS

In 2016, on the red carpet of the European premiere of the movie Tarzan, actress Margot Robbie asked her co-star, Alexander Skarsgård, to help her fix a problem with the back of her dress.

12. Kristen Stewart at the premiere of Twilight: Breaking Dawn Part 2

The nude dress Kristen Stewart wore for the premiere of Twilight: Breaking Dawn Part 2 may not have been her best choice. The actress wore a soft gold-colored corset dress paired with a sheer skirt, confidently showing off the skin underneath the translucent fabric.
